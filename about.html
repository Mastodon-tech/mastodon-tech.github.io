Our Journey!

In December 2015, Mastodon Ltd. (previously known as Mastodon Wash) started its journey with a waterless home car wash home service. It would export the products from abroad and serve it to its customers through its trained workforce.


Soon after a yearlong period of trial and error with the waterless car wash technique, Mastodon Wash diverted its services into regular car wash service in 2016 and only focused on recreating the Auto Detailing Industry in Bangladesh. Moving on with our ideas, while acquiring more customers, we have reached a milestone of top 100 business ideas at Startup Istanbul in 2017


Addition to our International recognition, Mastodon became Top 10 business ideas at Microsoft Young Bangla Summit in 2018 respectively.


By 2019, the company has validated both the products and its customers and it was ready to be commercially Launched on 30th August 2019 with our first commercial home car wash service at capitals Bashundhara Residential Area. August 30th 2019, our journey as a company begins.


On our very first year, by the end of Fiscal Year 2020, we have served a little less than 500 orders and have provided full-fledged home car services during the Pandemic-COVID-19. In the mean time we have received with the Bangabandhu Innovation Grant of BDT 1 million by the Government of Bangladesh and won multiple native and global recognitions in respective areas of the industry. Slowly but surely, we have survived another scare some year of the pandemic and by the end of FY 2021 we have served an approximate of 1800+ home car maintenance services and counting.


Mastodon has undertaken many steps to fight with the COVID-19 pandemic. On 2021, According to Startup Bangladesh and Startup Consortium of Bangladesh, Mastodon has showcased its innovations to the world through Prarambh- building a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and Startups in India.


On 2021, Mastodon Wash became Mastodon Ltd. With the change, the company has since added multiple utilities to its services, such as; car wash, car repair, car buy & sell and a dedicated marketplace for authentic car part and utilities. With a mission of recreating the Auto Detailing Industry in Bangladesh and making car maintenance easier than anything we are operating in the Bangladeshi market since 2015, or 2019 commercially.


Mission

Recreating the Auto Detailing Industry in Bangladesh.

Vision

Automate vehicle maintenance using tech and intelligence; cutting down the service hours.
